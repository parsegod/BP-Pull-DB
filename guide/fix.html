Get Started

How to Get Unstuck from 0/0

If you're currently facing the 0/0 bug and need to get back into the game, follow these steps carefully.

  1. 1

    Back to "Press A to Start" Screen

    You need to back out all the way to the "Press A to Start screen". Even if you still have the 0/0 glitch showing, do this. You'll notice that when you have the 0/0 bug, this "Press A to Start" screen often doesn't render in properly (90% of the time). However, when you back out from being loaded into the game, it renders in at the beginning no matter what. This difference is irrelevant for this guide.

  2. 2

    Close Your Game Normally

    Once you're on the "Press A to Start" screen, close your game normally.

  3. 3

    Remove from Quick Resume

    After closing the game, make sure to remove it from Quick Resume on your Xbox.

  4. 4

    Turn Console Off (DO NOT RESTART)

    Turn your console completely off. DO NOT RESTART your console.

  5. 5

    Wait 10 Minutes

    Wait for 10 minutes with your console turned off.

  6. 6

    Turn On and Log In with a Different Account

    After 10 minutes, turn your console back on and login with a different account. This should be an account that was *not* experiencing the 0/0 bug.
